The Full Job

Everything your driveway needs, start to finish

01

Edge the Driveway

We trim and clean along every edge — grass lines, garden beds, curbs — so nothing bleeds over when we seal.

02

Leaf Blow

Every corner gets blown out. Leaves, dirt, and loose debris are cleared before we do anything else.

03

Power Wash

We wash the full surface to pull up oil, grime, and anything stuck in the asphalt that would mess with adhesion.

04

Crack Filling

Cracks get filled before we seal — keeps water from working its way underneath and making things worse over time.

05

Sealcoating

Commercial-grade sealer applied to the full surface. Deep, even finish that protects against UV, water, and wear.
